Personality And Cognitive Functions

What are cognitive functions?

Put simply these are functions in our brains that determine our behaviours
and how we generally perceive life.They are eight in number.

You're in the right place so simply dive in.

One's core personality is determined by the cognitive functions whereby each
individual has four main cognitive functions at work. These keep developing throughut the person's lifetime
with their dominant function presenting itself in early childhood and the fourth or the inferior function presenting itself last in late 30s
if at all developed.

The eight functions include;

  • Extroverted Thinking
  • Introverted Intuition
  • Extroverted Sensing
  • Introverted Feeling
  • Extraverted Feeling
  • Introverted Thinking
  • Extroverted Intuition
  • Introverted Sensing
