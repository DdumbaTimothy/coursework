Who we are

Personalitee is a company that was started in 2000 at the turn of the new decade. With the change of the century, research on human psychology was also reaching new unprecedented heights championed by us here at Personalitee. Research and studies were becoming increasingly accurate and specific and therefore Personalitee specialised in the 16 Myers-Briggs personality types. We have been bringing clarity in lives and transforming livelihoods for 21 years now!
